Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-5p products is 28275383. The number of overall Sentinel-5p missing products consists of 25038452 images. This represents that a 100% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 0% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-5p products in the last 30 days is 28275383. The number of Sentinel-5p missing products during the last 30 days consists of 25048541 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.

Data ingestion#

In this section the time difference between sensing time and ingestion time at MET Norway is assessed. The ingestion time is the time at which a Sentinel product was downloaded to MET Norway BE and so, it is automatically available in at least one of the MET Norway FEs.
