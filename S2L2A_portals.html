Missing products¶

The overall total number of Sentinel-2 Level-2A products is 19337578. The number of overall Sentinel-2 Level-2A missing products consists of 17785946 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-2 level-2A products in the last 30 days is 19217014. The number of Sentinel-2 Level-2A missing products during the last 30 days consists of 17674645 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.

Data ingestion¶

In this section the time difference between sensing time and ingestion time at MET norway is assessed. The ingestion time is the time at which a Sentinel product was downloaded to MET Norway BE and so, it is automatically available in at least one of the MET Norway FEs.
