Finally, the total amount of disk space dedicated to the NBS project, including either products in SAFE and NetCDF formats, represents 5419 Tb.

Due to tracking the data ingested and produced for the NBS project in the last year it is possible to forecast the upcoming need for disk space. As long as data flows follows the same pattern than last year, in 6 months the total disk space will grow until 5684 Tb; while in 12 months it is forecast to become 6920 Tb.
