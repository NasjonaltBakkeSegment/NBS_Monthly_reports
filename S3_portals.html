Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-3 products is 4337890. The number of overall Sentinel-3 missing products consists of 1504164 images. This represents that a 700% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a -600% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-3 products in June is 629253. The number of Sentinel-3 missing products during June consists of 493069 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.

Data ingestion#

In this section the time difference between sensing time and ingestion time at MET Norway is assessed. The ingestion time is the time at which a Sentinel product was downloaded to MET Norway BE and so, it is automatically available in at least one of the MET Norway FEs.
