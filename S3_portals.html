Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-3 products is 5433944. The number of overall Sentinel-3 missing products consists of 2457381 images. This represents that a 500% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a -400% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-3 products in July is 1096054. The number of Sentinel-3 missing products during July consists of 953217 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.

Data ingestion#

In this section the time difference between sensing time and ingestion time at MET Norway is assessed. The ingestion time is the time at which a Sentinel product was downloaded to MET Norway BE and so, it is automatically available in at least one of the MET Norway FEs.
