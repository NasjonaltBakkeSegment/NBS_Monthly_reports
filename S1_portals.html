Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-1 products is 28275383. The number of overall Sentinel-1 missing products consists of 25503751 images. This represents that a 100% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 0% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-1 products in the last 30 days is 28275383. The number of Sentinel-1 missing products during the last 30 days consists of 25517028 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.
