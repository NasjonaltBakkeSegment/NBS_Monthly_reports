Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-1 products is 6878944. The number of overall Sentinel-1 missing products consists of 3761619 images. This represents that a 400% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a -300% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-1 products in August is 1445000. The number of Sentinel-1 missing products during August consists of 1304238 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.
