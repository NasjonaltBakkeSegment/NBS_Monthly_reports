Missing products#

The overall total number of Sentinel-1 products is 5433944. The number of overall Sentinel-1 missing products consists of 2457381 images. This represents that a 500% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a -400% was not included.

The total number of Sentinel-1 products in July is 1096054. The number of Sentinel-1 missing products during July consists of 953217 images. This represents that a 0% of the total was included in MET Norway DHR, while a 100% was not included.
